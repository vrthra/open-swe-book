# Chapter 1 — Open Resources

Free, openly‑licensed materials that reinforce this chapter. Types: 📘 open text · 🎓
course · 📄 primary source · 🎥 video.

## What software engineering is

- 🎓 **UC Berkeley — Engineering Software as a Service (ESaaS), ch. 1** —
  [saasbook.info](https://saasbook.info/). Motivates SE and agile from first principles.
- 📘 **MIT 6.031 Software Construction, "Reading 1"** —
  [web.mit.edu/6.031](http://web.mit.edu/6.031/www/sp21/classes/01-static-checking/).
  Frames all of SE around three goals: *safe from bugs, easy to understand, ready for
  change.*
- 📄 **SWEBOK Guide v4 (IEEE Computer Society)** —
  [computer.org body of knowledge](https://www.computer.org/education/bodies-of-knowledge/software-engineering).
  The discipline's knowledge areas at a glance.

## Complexity and defects

- 📄 F. Brooks, *No Silver Bullet* (essence vs. accident) — widely available as a PDF via
  library and university course pages; search "No Silver Bullet Brooks PDF".
- 📘 **MIT 6.031** readings on *static checking* and *testing* develop the
  fault‑vs‑failure and black‑box‑vs‑white‑box distinctions used here.

## Professional responsibility (used in §1.6)

- 📄 **ACM Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct** —
  [acm.org/code-of-ethics](https://www.acm.org/code-of-ethics).
- 📄 **Software Engineering Code of Ethics (ACM/IEEE‑CS)** —
  [computer.org/education/code-of-ethics](https://www.computer.org/education/code-of-ethics).
- 📄 N. Leveson & C. Turner, **"An Investigation of the Therac‑25 Accidents"**, *IEEE
  Computer* 26(7), 1993 —
  [PDF](https://web.stanford.edu/class/cs240/old/sp2014/readings/therac-25.pdf). The
  definitive account.
- 📄 **Volkswagen "defeat device"** — U.S. EPA Notice of Violation (Sept. 2015),
  [epa.gov](https://www.epa.gov/vw). Primary regulatory source.
